Status Slice Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

At Valtuni, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how Status Slice collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Status Slice collects information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Usage Data: We collect analytics data including app features used, session duration, and crash reports to improve the app experience.
  • Device Information: We collect device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and support purposes.
  • Payment Information: When you subscribe to premium features, payment is processed through Google Play Store. We do not store your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • To process subscriptions and manage premium features
  • To send you updates, notifications, and promotional materials (with your consent)
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize app performance
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and technical issues

Third-Party Services

Status Slice uses third-party services to provide and improve our app. These services may collect information as described in their own privacy policies.

  • Google Play Services: For app distribution, in-app purchases, and subscription management.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and advertising data to serve personalized ads.
  • Firebase Analytics: For understanding app usage and improving user experience.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For crash reporting and stability monitoring.

Advertising

Status Slice displays advertisements to free users. Our advertising partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Status Slice is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Status Slice after any updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
