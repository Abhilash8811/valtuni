Daily Killer Sudoku
Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Valtuni ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use Daily Killer Sudoku — a browser-based puzzle game. The short version: almost nothing leaves your device. Your game progress lives entirely in your browser's localStorage.

1. Information Collection and Use

✦ Daily Killer Sudoku stores all game data locally in your browser using localStorage. No game progress, puzzle states, or scores are ever transmitted to any server.

  • Game Progress (localStorage): Level completions, star ratings, solve times, unlocked levels, daily challenge streaks, and theme preferences are all stored locally in your browser. Clearing your browser data will reset your progress.
  • No Account Required: The game has no login, no registration, and no user profiles. We never ask for your name, email, or any personal information to play.
  • Puzzle Seeds: Levels are procedurally generated from a deterministic seed. No puzzle data is fetched from or sent to a server — all generation happens in your browser.

2. Third-Party Services (Advertising)

The game uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use information to serve ads:

  • Google AdMob: May collect your device's Advertising ID, IP address, browser type, and usage data to provide personalised advertisements. This is governed by Google's own privacy policy.
  • Opting Out: You can limit personalised advertising through your device's advertising settings, or by visiting adssettings.google.com.

For more information: Google Privacy Policy · AdMob Privacy FAQ

3. Log Data and Analytics

In the case of a bug or error, we may receive anonymous crash information through browser error reporting. This may include your browser type, operating system version, the page URL, and a stack trace. This data is entirely anonymous, never linked to your identity, and used exclusively to fix bugs.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

The game uses browser localStorage (not cookies) to save your game state. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party. You can clear it at any time through your browser's settings — though this will reset your progress.

5. Children's Privacy

Daily Killer Sudoku is a logic puzzle game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since no personal information is collected to play the game, there is no risk of inadvertent data collection from children. Advertisements served by AdMob may be subject to additional child-directed treatment controls.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective immediately upon posting to this page. We advise reviewing this page periodically to stay informed of any updates.
