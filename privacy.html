1. Information Collection and Use

✦ All Valtuni apps are designed to process your files locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your personal files to any server.

Across our apps, we may collect limited, anonymous information:

  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers — used only for crash reporting and stability improvements. Never linked to your identity.
  • Payment Information: All transactions are processed through Google Play Store. We do not store or have access to any payment details whatsoever.
  • Storage Access: Apps only access files you explicitly select within the app. Your other files are never accessed or scanned.

2. Third-Party Services

✦ Our apps use Google Play Services and Google AdMob which have their own privacy practices.

  • Google AdMob: Used to display ads. AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID and usage data to serve personalised ads. You can opt out in your Android device's advertising settings.
  • Google Play Billing: Handles all in-app purchases and Pro subscriptions securely. We never see or store your payment credentials.

For more information: Google Privacy Policy · Google Play Terms

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational security measures to protect the limited information we do collect. Since your files never leave your device, they remain as secure as your device itself. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete it. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page. Changes are effective immediately upon posting. We advise you to review this page periodically.
