Valtuni Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

At Valtuni, we value your trust. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information across our apps.

Status Slice Privacy Policy PDF Editor Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

Across our apps, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • Usage Data: Analytics data including app features used, session duration, and crash reports.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Payment Information: Processed through Google Play Store. We do not store payment details.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Process subscriptions and manage premium features
  • Send updates, notifications, and promotional materials (with consent)
  • Analyze usage patterns and optimize performance

Third-Party Services

Our apps use third-party services including Google Play Services, Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics.

Advertising

Our free apps display advertisements. Premium subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
