Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

At Valtuni, privacy isn't just a feature — it's the foundation of everything we build. This Privacy Policy explains how Status Slice handles your data. The short version: we don't collect any of it.

Information Collection

Status Slice is designed as a fully offline-first application. We want to be completely transparent about what this means for your privacy:

  • No personal information is collected. We do not ask for, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information — not your name, email, phone number, or location.
  • No analytics or usage data is gathered. We have no SDKs, no telemetry, and no hidden data pipelines running in the background.
  • No tracking of any kind. There are no cookies, advertising IDs, device fingerprinting, or any other tracking mechanisms embedded in the app.
  • No account creation required. Status Slice works immediately after installation with no sign-up, login, or registration process.

Data Storage

All data processing in Status Slice happens entirely on your device. Here's how storage works:

  • All media files remain on your device. Videos, audio files, and images you work with never leave your phone. They are not uploaded to any server or cloud service.
  • No server communication. Status Slice does not connect to external servers. All operations — splitting, trimming, compressing, merging, and audio extraction — are performed locally using your device's processor.
  • Local cache only. Any temporary files created during video processing are stored locally and can be cleared at any time through the app's built-in storage cleanup tool or your device's settings.
  • You have full control. Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device. No data persists on any external system because no data ever leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Status Slice operates completely independently without any third-party integrations. The app does not connect to, communicate with, or transmit data to any external service, API, or platform.

  • No advertising networks. Status Slice contains zero advertisements and no ad SDKs of any kind.
  • No analytics providers. We do not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Crashlytics, or any other analytics or crash reporting service.
  • No social media integrations. The app does not connect to any social platform. Sharing exported files is done entirely through your device's native sharing options.
  • No cloud services. No data is sent to AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, or any other cloud infrastructure. Everything stays on your phone.

Children's Privacy

Status Slice is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or any other users. Since the app functions entirely offline and does not collect any data whatsoever, there is no risk of children's information being gathered or stored beyond what is already on their device.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used Status Slice, rest assured that no personal data has been transmitted or stored by Valtuni. All media files remain on the child's device and are fully under the parent's control.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal and regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.

Since Status Slice's core privacy commitment — no data collection, no tracking, no server communication — is fundamental to our product philosophy, any changes to this policy will continue to uphold these principles. We will not introduce data collection features to an app built on the promise of complete privacy.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of Status Slice after any updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
