PDF Editor Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Valtuni ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use the PDF Editor mobile application (the "App").

1. Information Collection and Use

PDF Editor is designed to process your documents locally on your device whenever possible.

  • Document Files (PDFs): The App requires read and write permissions to your device storage to view, edit, merge, split, sign, and compress PDF documents. Basic document processing occurs locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or transmit your personal documents to our servers unless a specific feature requires server-side processing.
  • Storage Permissions: We only access the specific files you choose to process within the App and save the resulting files back to your local storage.

2. Third-Party Services

While we do not collect personal data directly through the core functionality of the App, we use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: The App uses AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use your device's Advertising ID, network information, and usage data to provide personalized ads.
  • Google Play Billing: For PDF Editor Pro subscriptions and in-app purchases, transactions are securely handled by Google Play Billing. We do not collect, process, or have access to your personal payment information (such as credit card details).

For more information on how these third parties handle your data, please review their respective privacy policies: Google Privacy Policy and Google Play Terms of Service.

3. Log Data and Analytics

In the case of an error or app crash, we may collect data and information (through third-party products like Google Analytics or Crashlytics) on your phone called Log Data. This Log Data may include information such as your device Internet Protocol ("IP") address, device name, operating system version, the configuration of the app when utilizing our service, the time and date of your use of the service, and other statistics. This data is entirely anonymous and used exclusively to improve the stability of the App.

4. Security

We value your trust in using our App. Since your documents are processed locally on your device, they remain as secure as your device itself. However, remember that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Children's Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our accessible records. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
